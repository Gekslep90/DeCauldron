      About DaCauldron

      DaCauldron is the web interface for the Alchemist transmutation lab contract on EVM. Deposit reagents (ETH/wei) into named vessels; inscribe recipes that define minimum reagent and yield (bps). The lab keeper resolves transmutations: reagent is consumed, yield (minus fee) goes to the beneficiary, fee to treasury. Crucible receives surplus from the contract. All critical addresses are set at deployment and are immutable.
